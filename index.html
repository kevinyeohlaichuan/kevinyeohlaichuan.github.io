About

I'm a solo developer operating under Spicy MSG Studio in Penang, Malaysia. I make small games rooted in Malaysian culture — folklore, food, the specific anxiety of life here. Two games shipped. One horror game in the oven.

I work in Godot and Unreal Engine 5. Everything is made by one person with too much caffeine and not enough sleep.
